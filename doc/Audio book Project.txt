**Audio book Project**

1\. Playback & Audio Control

_Core listening experience features._

- - Smart Resume: Intelligently rewinds audio by 2-5 seconds after a long pause or interruption so you reorient yourself with the context.
    - Silence Trimmer: Automatically detects and removes long pauses between words or sentences to shorten total listening time.
    - Variable Speed: Granular speed control (0.5x to 3.5x) with pitch correction to prevent "chipmunk" voices.
    - Volume Boost: Digital volume amplification (up to 3x) for older or quiet recordings.
    - Vocal EQ: specialized equalizer presets (e.g., "Voice Boost," "Reduce Bass") to make speech clearer in noisy environments.
    - Sleep Timer: Auto-stop after 15/30/60 minutes or "End of Chapter."
    - Shake-to-Reset Timer: Extend the sleep timer by shaking the phone without unlocking the screen or opening eyes.
    - Car Mode: A simplified driver-safe interface with massive buttons for Play, Pause, and 30s Rewind.
    - Smart Rewind: Rewind buttons that vary based on content length (e.g., 10s back, 30s forward).
    - Cross-Device Sync: Seamlessly continue listening at the exact second you left off on another device (Phone → Tablet → Web).
- Repeat Button (repeat chapter or book X times, or infinitely)
- Offline Listening / Download (save to device for without internet)
- Streaming Quality Options (low/medium/high bandwidth)
- Audio Normalization (equalizes volume across different books)
- Pitch Correction (prevents chipmunk voices at higher speeds)

2\. Content & Immersion

_Features that enhance the format beyond just audio._

- - Synced Text : Highlights the text sentence-by-sentence on the screen as it is spoken.
    - Immersive Audio: Support for Spatial Audio / Dolby Atmos for 3D soundscapes (dramatized audio).
    - Author Video Messages: Short video intros or "behind the scenes" clips from the author embedded in the book.
    - Visual Comments (Danmu): User comments scroll across the screen at specific timestamps (social layer).

3\. BOOKMARKS, NOTES & HIGHLIGHTS

_Tools for marking and remembering important moments._

- Bookmarks (single-tap to mark a timestamp)
- Clips with Custom Lengths (create bookmarks of custom durations, not just single points)
- Notes on Bookmarks (attach text annotations to any bookmark)
- Titles for Clips (name custom clips for easy identification)
- Highlights (color-coded selection of passages: yellow, blue, green, etc.)
- Multi-Color Highlighting (different colors for different types of notes)
- Note Preview (see all notes without leaving the player)
- Bookmark Sorting Options (organize by timestamp, chapter name, or creation date)
- Bookmark Folder/Categories (group clips by topic or section)
- Export Bookmarks & Notes (export as text file or PDF after returning library book)
- Edit Clip Duration (adjust the start/end point of a saved clip after creation)
- Delete Bookmarks (remove individual clips)
- Jump to Bookmark (instantly skip to saved position)
- Play Specific Clip (play just the bookmarked segment without skipping to full context)

3\. Library & Organization

_Tools for managing large collections._

- - Cloud Library: Unlimited storage of purchased titles in the cloud, not taking up device space.
    - Local File Import: Ability to upload and play your own MP3/M4B files alongside the store catalog.
    - Smart Tags/Shelves: Custom user-created lists (e.g., "Favorites," "DNF," "Road Trip") beyond just "Finished" and "Unfinished."
    - Family Sharing: Share access to purchased books with 4-5 family members via a linked household account.
    - Multi-Library Search: Link multiple public library cards to search all catalogs simultaneously for a specific title.
    - Series Grouping: Automatically groups books from the same series together in correct chronological order.
    - Reading Log: Detailed stats on listening time, streaks, and total books finished per year.
- Status Tracking (mark books as New, Started, Finished, or custom status)
- Progress Percentage (see how much of a book you've completed)
- Last Listened Date (timestamp of most recent listen)
- DRM-Free Downloads (own the raw MP3 file forever, no restrictions)
- Local Back-up (manually export library as backup)
- Wishlist/Want to Read (save books to read later)
- Recently Listened (quick access to books you're actively reading)
- Search Filters (filter by author, genre, language, duration, rating)
- Sort Options (alphabetical, date added, popularity, rating)
- Collections by Genre (auto-categorized shelves)

4\. Discovery & AI

_How users find their next book._

- - AI Narrator Option: Option to switch between a human narrator and a synthetic AI voice (often cheaper or for obscure titles).
    - Emotional Search: Search for books based on "mood" (e.g., "uplifting," "scary," "tear-jerker") rather than just genre.
    - Samples / Trailers: dedicated feed of short 2-5 minute audio clips to "audition" books before buying.
    - Countdown Pages: Pre-save upcoming releases to get a notification the second they launch.
    - Similar Title Match: "If you liked X, you'll like Y" based on listening patterns, not just sales data.
- AI-Powered Personalized Recommendations (Graph Neural Networks analyzing listening patterns)
- Microgenre Tags ("Romantic Suspense," "Police Procedural," "Cozy Mystery")
- Spotlight / Featured Books (curated editorial picks)
- Top Lists by Category ("Best Fiction," "Best Non-Fiction," "Most Popular")
- New Releases Section (latest additions to catalog)
- Trending Now (books popular this week/month)
- Award-Winning Filters (filter by Pulitzer, Booker Prize, etc.)
- Audiobook-Only Originals (exclusive content not available elsewhere)
- Content Metadata Rich (title, author, narrator, duration, release date, synopsis)
- Author Follow (get notified of new releases from favorite authors)
- Series Follow (get notified of new books in favorite series)
